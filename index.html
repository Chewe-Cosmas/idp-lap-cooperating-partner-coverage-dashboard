Purpose

This dashboard shows where cooperating partners are supporting or considering support for the review of Integrated Development Plans (IDPs) and Local Area Plans (LAPs). It is intended to promote coordinated planning, improve visibility of partner commitments, reduce duplication of effort, and support evidence-based decision-making.
